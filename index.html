About

Music for quiet rooms and warm evenings

Swayly blends slow jazz and ambient piano textures, made for calm rooms, late-night desks and warm light. Each track lives between careful harmonies, brushed cymbals, upright piano and soft-tone choices, keeping the mood gentle and unhurried.
