Licensing & usage

For cafés, studios, podcasts, YouTube channels and creative projects that would like to use Swayly tracks, please include:

  • A short description of your project
  • Where the music will be used (online / offline / both)
  • Approximate dates and duration

Send an email and we’ll respond with licensing options that fit quiet, long-term use rather than loud campaigns.
